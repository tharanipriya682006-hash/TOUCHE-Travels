About Us

A small team that treats your trip like our own.

TOUCHÉ Travels started in Chennai in 2014 with a simple idea: travel planning should feel like a conversation with a well-travelled friend, not a checkout page.

Home/About

Our Story

From one desk in Chennai to 3,400+ journeys planned.

TOUCHÉ Travels began as a two-person desk helping friends and family plan honeymoons that didn't feel like everyone else's honeymoon. Over a decade later, we're still doing the same thing — just for a lot more people.

Every itinerary that leaves our office has been walked through by a planner who has either been there themselves or has spoken at length to someone who has. No copy-pasted routes, no commission-driven upsells.

'14Founded

Built on referrals, not ads.

Most of our travellers come to us because a friend told them to. We'd like to keep it that way.
